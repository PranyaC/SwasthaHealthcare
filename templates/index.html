Delivering Cutting-Edge Medical Care with Compassion

Our Mission

In an age where malpractice threatens the lives of our community, we aim to create a safe environment by emphasizing precautions, safety, and welfare of our patients. We do that by striving to lower medical errors, reduce readmission rates, and educate our patients on how to live a healthy lifestyle.

To rekindle our patient’s lost hope

Because not all wounds are visible, we, are dedicated to healing the unseen. We do that by helping our patients overcome mental health challenges. We’re committed to being confidential, sympathetic, and approachable in our communications and treatment.

What Makes Us Stand Apart

Wheelchair Accessibility

Designed rationally patient related spaces to help enable patients march towards self sufficiency and independence. All our facilities are special needs friendly and make everyone feel welcome.

Prompt Ambulance Services

No time is wasted for situations where every second counts. Our vast fleet is always at the ready with fully stocked primary care supplies so that you get the best care possible swiftly.

World Renowned Programs

We are globally renowned for our outreach programs as well as our medical advancement programs in cancer research, cardiovascular ailment research and our mental wellness programs.

State-of-the-art Facilities

All of our centres are developed with a green and sustainable mindset. We procure the best technology has to offer to ensure skilled workers have all the right tools available to them to give you the best care possible.

Why We Are The Best

We have passionate and hardworking doctors, nurses and support staff with the finest medical training who use cutting-edge technological advancements to improve the health and wellness of all their patients.

About Us

Our Founding Team and Pillars of Strength



Dr. Naresh Trehan

Dr. Trehan is an Indian cardiovascular and cardiothoracic surgeon. He graduated from King George Medical College, Lucknow, India, and went on to practice at New York University Medical Center, Manhattan USA.



Dr. H.S. Chhabra

Dr. Chhabra is one of the coveted & most experienced surgeons in the field of Spine Surgery in India. He is well known for pioneering Laser and Endoscopic disc surgery in India.

Dr. Mohamed Rela

Dr. Rela is a renowned surgeon known for his expertise in liver transplantation and hepatopancreatobiliary surgery. He has performed more than 3000 liver transplant surgeries so far.
